Ally

Ally is an expert app for job seekers searching for quick industry specific insights and actionable advice when applying for the job of their dreams.

This was a project that I developed during my User Experience Design course at Career Foundry. The work started in January 2020. The deliverables were ready for handoff in May same year.

My approach

During the course of the project I was following user-centered design framework that allowed me to organize and plan my tasks without losing focus on the problem and the user.
